# Boost Pad

## Overview[[edit](Boost_Pad?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Boost Pad** its a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scrap and bateries. When placed in the base a player can run over it to gain high walk speed for 15 seconds. When a player recives the buff the Boost Pad won't be available to use for 5 seconds.
