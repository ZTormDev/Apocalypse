## Overview[[edit](Irradiated_Zone?action=edit&section=1.md)]
File:Irradiated_Zone.png.mdIrradiated Zone

This area is a toxic zone on the map that is highlighted green. It will have stronger [Zombies](Zombies.md) and damage players who enter without equipping a gas mask. If a player enters the player without a mask a message will appear on the screen that says "This air is radioactive! You need a [Gas Mask](Accessories.md) to survive. Loot will be better in this area. The [Nuclear Reactor](Nuclear_Reactor.md) is also located here.
