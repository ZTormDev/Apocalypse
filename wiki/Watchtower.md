# Watchtower

## Overview[[edit](Watchtower?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Watchtower** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scraps and allows a player to shoot zombies from high ground.

## Notes[[edit](Watchtower?action=edit&section=2.md)]
