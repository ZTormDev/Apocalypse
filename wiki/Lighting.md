# Lighting

## Overview[[edit](Lighting?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The hue of the lighting changes slightly when entering different biomes. The difference is minimal but is enough to notice.

## Irradiated Zone[[edit](Lighting?action=edit&section=2.md)]

When entering this zone/biome, the lighting will change from clear to a yellowish hue. When leaving this zone/biome, the lighting will switch back to being clear.

## Military Zone[[edit](Lighting?action=edit&section=3.md)]

When entering this zone/biome, the lighting will change from clear to a greenish hue. When leaving this zone/biome, the lighting will switch back to being clear.
