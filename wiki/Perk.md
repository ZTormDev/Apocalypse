## Overview[[edit](Perk?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Perks are additional boosts that can be equipped to [classes](Class.md). They can be obtained by purchasing and rolling from the ***Perk Crate*** for 100 or roll 3 perks at once with 300. Each roll grants a random perk, and only one perk can be equipped per class. Equipping a new perk will permanently remove and destroy the previously equipped perk.

File:PerkCrateRoll.png.mdDefault Perk Crate UI

## Non-Gambling Exclusive Perk Shop[[edit](Perk?action=edit&section=2.md)]

Players who are included in countries where gambling and lootboxes are restricted, do not access the random perk system on the ***Perk Crate***. Instead, they access the Perk Shop via ***Perk Crate*** that displays four purchasable perks that refreshes every 24 hours. Each perk can be purchased directly using [Emeralds](Currencies#Emeralds.md)  at a fixed price depending on the rarity.

## Rarities[[edit](Perk?action=edit&section=3.md)]

These are the probabilities that tells how likely you are to obtain a perk when purchasing to open the **Perk Crate.**

- Common: 58%

- **Rare: 30%**

- **Epic: 10%**

- **Legendary: 2%**
