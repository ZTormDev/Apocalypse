## Overview[[edit](Stats?action=edit&section=1.md)]

These are stats the player has. The bars appear on the left side of the players screen. There is a green bar for Health and a orange bar for Hunger 

### Health[[edit](Stats?action=edit&section=2.md)]

This is a green bar on the screen that denotes how much health a player has. When the player takes damage, the health bar will go down. When the player heals, the health bar will go up. Once a player's **hunger** bar completely depletes, the player will begin taking damage to their health bar.

### Hunger[[edit](Stats?action=edit&section=3.md)]

This is an orange bar on the screen that denotes how much hunger a player has. When the player is idle, walks, sprints, or moves the **hunger** bar will deplete. Once the player's hunger bar depletes, the player will begin taking damage to their health bar and takes away the ability to sprint when starving. Players can eat [Carrots](Consumables#Hunger.md) and [MREs](Consumables#Hunger.md) to refill their hunger bar.

### Speed[[edit](Stats?action=edit&section=4.md)]

This is how fast a player moves in the game. Players can sprint, use the [Boost Pad](Boost_Pad.md) or use [Compound S](Consumables#Compound_S.md) to increase their speed. There are also classes that give speed buffs as a passive ability.

### Damage[[edit](Stats?action=edit&section=5.md)]

This is how much damage a player can do in game. Certain classes like [Assassin](Class#Assassin.md) add buffs to damage as a passive.
