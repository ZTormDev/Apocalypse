## Overview[[edit](Enemies?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Enemies are hostile NPCs found throughout the map that will attack players on sight. As players explore farther from their [Base](Base.md), they will encounter a variety of Zombies and Bandits types, each progressively stronger and more dangerous than the last as you progress through your days.

## **Zombie Types**[[edit](Enemies?action=edit&section=2.md)]

File:Default_zombie.png.mdThe Zombie.

A walking corpse.

- The default, normal zombie.

- Walks toward the closest target.

- Deals low damage.

- Has low amount of HP.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Crawler.png.mdThe Crawler.

A zombie with no legs.

- Will crawl toward the closest target, and can climb obstacles.

- Deals low damage.

- Has little more HP than the default zombie.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Runner.png.mdThe Runner.

A fast zombie with exposed flesh and bones.

- Runs toward the closest target.

- Deals low, but fast damage.

- Has the same HP as the default zombie.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Bloater.png.mdThe Bloater.

A tall zombie with a bloated belly.

- Walks toward the closest target.

- Deals low damage (normal attack).

- Has quite amount of HP.

- Explodes on death, dealing high damage to everything in the surrounding (zombies, structures, scrap piles, players, and survivors).

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:SpitterZombie.png.mdThe Spitter.

A green, translucent zombie that spits acid.

- Runs slowly at the closest target.

- Deals low-medium damage.

- Has the same HP as the default runner.

- Will back off if its target gets too close, and will use its hands to attack.

- Spits acid at the closest target. Players will have a "slowed" effect if they are hit with this.

- Can spit at the target even at high position (e.g spitting players that are standing on a watchtower).

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Riot.png.mdThe Riot.

A zombie dressed in Riot Gear with a riot shield and a baton.

- Walks toward the closest target.

- Deals medium damage with the baton.

- Has quite amount of HP.

- Can be damaged by shooting through shield's gap with a high damage [gun](Weapons#Guns.md) like [Rifle](Weapons#Guns.md).

- The shield can block bullets that have low penetration.

- The shield can be destroyed by hitting them with [melee](Weapons#tabbet-Melee.md) weapons or shooting with [guns](Weapons#tabbet-Melee.md).

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Phaser.png.mdThe Phaser.

A ghost-like zombie that can pass through anything.

- Walks or float toward the closest target.

- Deals low-medium damage.

- Has quite amount of HP.

- Can pass through objects like gates, barbed wire, fences, etc.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Hazmat.png.mdThe Hazmat.

A radioactive zombie wearing a hazmat suit.

- Runs toward the closest target (almost the same speed as the Runner).

- Deals medium damage.

- Has quite more HP.

- Can sometimes drop a Gas Mask, which allows players to go into the [Irradiated Zone](Irradiated_Zone.md) safely.

- Spawns in the [Irradiated Zone](Irradiated_Zone.md), [Nuclear Reactor](Nuclear_Reactor.md), and during the night horde.

File:ScreamerZombie.png.mdThe Screamer.

A strange, white zombie with long, sharp claws.

- Runs aggresively fast toward the closest target, and can climb obstacles.

- Deals high damage.

- Has significant amount of HP.

- Will scream and alert other zombies if the target gets close enough.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:Muscle.png.mdThe Muscle.

An orange, aggressive bulky zombie.

- Runs toward the closest target (less fast than the Runner).

- Deals medium-high damage.

- Has high amount of HP.

- Will throw a boulder, and pounce/jump on the closest target.

- Always spawns across the [Map](Map.md), during night horde, and [Blood Moon](Events#tabber-Blood_Moon.md).

File:ShadowMuscle.png.mdThe Shadow Muscle.

An dark, aggressive bulky zombie emitting a shadowy aura.

- Runs toward the closest target (less fast than the Runner).

- Deals medium-high damage.

- Has high amount of HP (little more HP than the Muscle).

- Will throw a boulder, and pounce/jump on the closest target.

- This zombie is a variation of the Muscle, which behaves similarly, but emits a shadowy aura.

- It is called the "Shadow Muscle" by players, although its in-game name is "Muscle" when pinged

- Spawns by opening the [Mystery Box](Mystery_Box.md)

File:Elemental.png.mdThe Elemental.

A zombie covered in flames.

- Runs slowly toward the closest target.

- Deals medium damage.

- Has significant amount of HP.

- Its attack will leave a burning effect that deals overtime damage to the player for 6 seconds.

- Immune to fire damage from [Molotov](Weapons#Molotov.md) and [Flamethrower](Weapons#Flamethrower.md).

- Spawns at night after day 80, and at [Supply Drops](Supply_Drops.md).

File:Electriczombie.png.mdThe Electrified.

An electrified zombie with electric aura and glowing eyes.

- Runs toward the closest target.

- Deals medium damage.

- Has significant amount of HP.

- Can dodge attacks every couple of seconds.

- Gives the Shocked effect that slows down players when hit.

- Exclusively spawns at the [Power Plant](Locations#tabber-Power_Plant.md).

File:Skeleton.png.mdThe Skeleton.

A heavily deformed zombie with a more noticeable exposed skeleton.

- Limited zombie.

- Runs toward the closest target.

- Deals low-medium damage.

- Has the same amount of HP as the Runner.

- This zombie is a variation of the Runner, which has no difference except the looks.

- Located in the [Blood Tomb](Events#tabber-Blood_Tomb_Raid.md) during the [Blood Harvest](Events#tabber-Blood_Harvest.md) Event.

- Can no longer be encountered due to the [Blood Harvest](Events#tabber-Blood_Harvest.md) event's conclusion

### Notes[[edit](Enemies?action=edit&section=3.md)]

Default zombies—including crawlers and runners may appear wearing police uniforms, hospital gowns, or construction outfits. These variations are purely cosmetic and do not grant any special abilities.

- Runs toward the closest target with aggressive and exceptional speed.

- Deals very high damage.

- Has double the amount of HP a normal Screamer has.

- Dark skintone with red eyes.

- Scream ability that alerts nearby hordes.

- Similar to the Night Hunter the Aberrant Screamer can get stunned by ranged weapons and melees.

- Variant starts spawning at day 250+.

File:PhaserSpecter.png.mdThe Phaser Specter.

A black ghost with long black hair and a glowing blue eye.

- Usually spawns at the Forest.

- Becomes completely invisible to players further than 50 studs away.

- Deals true damage.

- Attack speed increased

- Attack range increased

File:Muscle_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Muscle.

- Deals high damage.

File:Screamer_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Screamer.

A pale Screamer with long black claws and layered orange fungi across its torso. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

- Deals high, rapid damage.

File:Runner_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Runner.

A fast Runner with blocky, bone-exposed arms encrusted with orange bracket mushrooms. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

- Deals fast, medium damage.

File:Bloater_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Bloater.

A swollen Bloater variant covered in orange spores. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

- Walks toward the closest target.

- Has a significant amount of HP.

File:Riot_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Riot.

A Riot zombie wearing a black helmet and vest, using a circular fungal cluster as a shield. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

File:Crawler_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Crawler.

A legless green Crawler zombie dragging its torso, heavily covered in orange spore patches. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

- Crawls quickly and can climb obstacles.

File:Frost.png.mdThe Frost Elemental.

A Frozen version of the Elemental zombie which is covered in flames, looks like the normal elemental just a frozen tone and color. Can be seen spawning in the winter season.

- Every time you get hit you are chilled for 8 seconds (resets timer if you are already chilled and get hit again).

- Deals same damage as the normal Elemental zombie.

File:NightHunter.png.mdThe Night Hunter.
File:NightHunter-not.png.mdNight Hunter alert.

A dark zombie with a shadowy aura and long, sharp claws.

- Runs aggresively fast toward the closest target, and can climb obstacles (as fast as the Screamer.

- Deals high damage.

- Has very high amount of HP.

- It will run away after hitting the player once, and backs off if the player tries to get close, making it harder to attack using [Melee Weapons](Weapons.md).

- Dealing enough damage to the Night Hunter temporarily stuns it, allowing you to run.

- Will spawn at least one for every player being hunted (e.g 2 players being hunted by at least 2 Night Hunters).

- This type of zombie will spawn only when a player stays out of the base for long enough during the night.

File:Brute.png.mdThe Brute.

A giant muscular zombie. Can be found around the map and will spawn during [Blood Moons](Events#Blood_Moon.md).

- Can stomp and damage buildings, and throw zombies at you

- HP scales based on player count

File:ExperimentZombie.png.mdThe Experiment.

A massive green zombie with a laser cannon as its right arm.

- Can lunge and shoot laser cannon at the closest target

- HP scales with player count

- Drops the [Blue Alien Crystal](Looting#Blue_Alien_Crystal.md) once defeated.

- Located in the [Overgrown Nuclear Reactor](Overgrown_Nuclear_Reactor?action=edit&redlink=1.md).

### Notes[[edit](Enemies?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- Since the [Forest (Part 1)](Changelog#Forest_(Part_1).md) Update, this boss was removed along with the [Old Nuclear Reactor](Nuclear_Reactor.md) and the [Irradiated Zone](Irradiated_Zone.md).

- This boss was added back along with the new [Overgrown Nuclear Reactor](Overgrown_Nuclear_Reactor?action=edit&redlink=1.md).

File:Exterminator.png.mdThe Exterminator.

A large orange-red zombie equipped with a [Flamethrower](Weapons#Flamethrower.md) and a [Minigun](Weapons#Minigun.md). It spawns in the [Military Base Raid](Military_Base_Raid.md). 

- Drops a [Minigun](Weapons#Minigun.md) or a [Flamethrower](Weapons#Flamethrower.md) once defeated

- Can lunge, shoot minigun and flamethrower at the closest target, and summon muscles

- Has extremely high HP

- HP scales based on player count

- Drops a [Orange Alien Crystal](Looting#Orange_Alien_Crystal.md) once defeated.

File:SkeletonKing.png.mdThe Skeleton King.

A large skeleton equipped with a sword and a scepter.

- This boss was located inside the [Blood Tomb](Locations#tabber-Blood_Tomb.md) and was a limited boss during the [Blood Harvest](Events#tabber-Blood_Harvest.md) Event.

- Once defeated, it rewards every player on the server with 20 Sealed Blood Gems, which are directly placed in the inventory (escaping is required to claim the Blood Gems).

- Can no longer be encountered after [Blood Harvest](Events#tabber-Blood_Harvest.md) event's conclusion

File:Harbinger.jpeg.mdThe Harbinger.

A large wood-like zombie with a staff. This boss is located inside the [Cave](Cave.md).

- It summons Wooden Zombies when first discovered, and continues to do so until defeat.

- Can inflict the Blight effect, which continuously takes damage from the player until the effect ends or the player dies.

- Rewards 50 .

- Drops a [Green Alien Crystal](Looting#Green_Alien_Crystal.md) once defeated.

File:Brute_Infested.png.mdThe Infested Brute.

A massive brown titan boss with orange wing-like fungal growths sprouting from its back. Can be seen spawning in the infestation nodes after activating [Hard Mode](Hard_Mode.md).

- HP scales heavily based on player count.

- Can stomp and damage buildings, and throw zombies at you.
