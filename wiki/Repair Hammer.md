# Repair Hammer

## Overview[[edit](Repair_Hammer?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Repair Hammer** is a tool players can use to move structures, deconstruct structures, and repair structures. It can be crafted on [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) **Level 1** with scrap. 

File:RepairHammer.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Repair_Hammer?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- The [Engineer](Class#Engineer.md) Class has a repair hammer that repairs at 2x speed compared to the normal Repair Hammer.
