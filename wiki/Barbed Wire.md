# Barbed Wire

## Overview[[edit](Barbed_Wire?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Barbed Wire** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap, it deals damage and slows down [Zombies](Zombies.md) trying to pass through. It will not harm players or [Survivors](Survivors.md).

## Notes[[edit](Barbed_Wire?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)
