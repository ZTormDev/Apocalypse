## Hotbar[[edit](Day_1?action=edit&section=2.md)]

Your hotbar can store things such as [Weapons](Weapons.md), [Medical Items](Consumables.md), [Compound I](Consumables.md), [Compound R](Consumables.md), [Compound S](Consumables.md), and more. You can't store items such as Food, Scrap, and Batteries in the Hotbar. However you can store them in your [Backpack](Accessories.md).

Your Hotbar can be filled with up to 10 different items. They can be switched around to suit your personal preference when playing.

## Generator[[edit](Day_1?action=edit&section=3.md)]
File:Level_1.png.mdGenerator with its level above it 

The generator has 6 levels, with **Level 6** as the maximum level. The range of the [map](Map.md) also increases as the generator levels up. Players can upgrade the generator using [Fuel](Looting.md), [Refined Fuel](Looting.md), or [Nuclear Fuel](Looting.md).

There are 2 main things regarding the generator:

- If the generator runs out of fuel, zombie hordes that spawn during nights become enraged, increasing their strength and HP.

- If the generator gets destroyed by zombies, it'll automatically fix itself after 1 to 3 nights.

## Basic Survival[[edit](Day_1?action=edit&section=5.md)]
File:Crafting-table.png.mdCrafting Table

1. [Looting](Looting.md) [Crates](Looting.md), [Barrels](Looting.md) and [Buildings](Locations.md) provide players with [Weapons](Weapons.md), [Ammo](Weapons.md), [Consumables](Consumables.md), and [Backpacks](Accessories.md)

2. Build and fortify a base using the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md)

3. Survive waves (difficulty increases over time)

4. Watch for [Supply Drops](Events.md) and [Blood Moons](Events.md) (every 5 days)

5. Progress towards Day 100 for escaping, or continue to see how long you last!

## Key Tips[[edit](Day_1?action=edit&section=6.md)]

- The [Hospital](Locations.md) and [Police Station](Locations.md) have better loot but heavier zombie presence

- [Rain](Events.md) resets [Zombies](Zombies.md) and Crates when it ends - use it to re-loot and stock up

- Crafting scales in cost based on the number of players in the game - plan builds early
