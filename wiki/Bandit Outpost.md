# Bandit Outpost

## Overview[[edit](Bandit_Outpost?action=edit&section=1.md)]

This is an outpost where non-zombie NPC’s spawn. They are hostile and will shoot or hit you with melee weapons if you get close. It is highlighted orange on the map. This area will have crates and the **Radio Part** which is needed to fix the [Radio Tower](Locations#Radio_Tower.md) if you want to [Escape](Escape.md). The area is also filled with [Crates](Looting.md), [Emerald Crates](Currencies.md), and stand-alone [Loot](Looting.md).
