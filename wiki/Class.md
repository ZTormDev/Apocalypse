# Class

## Overview[[edit](Class?action=edit&section=1.md)]

These are characters that can be bought using Emeralds . There are obtainable classes and limited classes. Each one gives certain powerups and abilities. All classes are guaranteed to spawn in-game with a [Bat](Weapons#tabber-Bat.md), regardless whether they have starter items or not.

To buy a class, you have to go to the tent—which is located in the lobby, then talk to the Class Vendor (the soldier NPC) by pressing **E** on PC . By doing so, you will see the Class Shop displayed on your screen. Another way is by clicking the **Classes** button located on the left side of your screen.

Having trouble picking your class? Check [Class Tierlist](Guides.md).

### Details[[edit](Class?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- **Level 2 (Bounce):** AKA 'Richochet', Bullets from non-auto guns bounce between enemies on penetration (Max 3).

  - Bullets will ricochet/bounce off to 3 nearby enemies from 1 target, meaning, you'll be hitting 4 targets at the same time.

- **Quickdraw:** Rapidly fire your revolver, automatically headshotting a zombie on your screen 6 times (15s CD).

  - [Press X on PC] Quickdraw fires a barrage of 6 consecutive headshots (6 bullets max, directly uses ammo reserve.)

  - Heavy Revolver auto reloads after using Quickdraw.

  - Gain 'Deadeye' status effect with 5s duration after getting a headshot kill with any non-auto guns (except [Grenade Launcher](Weapons#tabber-Grenade_Launcher.md) and [Explosive Crossbow](Weapons#tabber-Explosive_Crossbow.md)).

  - Using Quickdraw while having no pistol ammo will activate it but will not fire.

  - Quickdraw only works with Heavy Revolver.

#### Notes[[edit](Class?action=edit&section=4.md)]

- There is currently no visual indicator for how much 'Deadeye' stacks in-game.

#### Remarks[[edit](Class?action=edit&section=5.md)]

- The Outlaw is a glass-cannon class; survivability is tied to maintaining "Precision Stacks"(Deadeye status effect) to keep your Dodge Chance high and movement speed up.

- The Level 2 ricochet mechanic makes the Heavy Revolver exceptionally effective against minor hordes.

- Quickdraw is a reliable skill for eliminating high-priority targets like [Brutes](Enemies#tabber-Brute.md) and [Experiment](Enemies#tabber-Experiment.md).

### Details[[edit](Class?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- **Stalk (Ability):** Turn invisible and gain movement speed for 7 seconds (2m CD, each kill reduce CD by 3s). Enhance the next Bloodthirster attack with a wide slash capable of dealing large damage to enemies hit. Each kill with the ability heals the user with small HP. The first kill after activing the skill while in **Stalk** state—will reduce its cooldown by 60s.

- **Bleed (Passive):** Enemies hit with the [Bloodthirster](Weapons#tabber-Bloodthirster.md) will bleed for 6 seconds (works with other melee weapons). After killing a bleeding enemy, the bleed will be passed onto another nearby enemy that doesn't yet have the bleeding effect (only works if the class is Level 2).

- **Enhanced Stats (Blood Moon Passive):** The Stalker receives additional movement speed. Gain additional damage and attack speed on [Bloodthirster](Weapons#tabber-Bloodthirster.md) (only works if the class is Level 2).

- **Stack (Passive):** Each kill with the [Bloodthirster](Weapons#tabber-Bloodthirster.md) gives it 1% damage, and +1 maximum HP to the user, and the [Bloodthirster](Weapons#tabber-Bloodthirster.md) will glow Weapons#Bloodthirster.md and emit a bloody aura when it reaches a maximum of 70 stacks (stacks will reset each day).
