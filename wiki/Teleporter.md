# Teleporter

## Overview[[edit](Teleporter?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Teleporter** is an building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap, batteries and a zombie heart. It can be placed anywhere on the map. When more than 2 of them are placed player are able to interact with them to get teleported to another teleporter.
