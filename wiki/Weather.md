# Weather

## Overview[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

There are currently two types of weather (weather and season) in the game. Both having different types and effects.

### Weathers[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]
FogRainThunderstorm

File:Foggy.png.mdFoggy Day

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- A weather condition where the surroundings look blurry or humid, which hinders the visibility of players.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Fog is permanent and causes low visibility.

File:Rain.png.mdRainy afternoon

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- The rain is another weather condition where there are downpour of water droplets, and also the fog.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- This is a random event that last 1 day and happens several times during the game.
File:RainStart.png.md

- After the Rain ends, [breakables](Looting#tabber-Breakables.md) (except [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md)), and [zombies](Enemies#tabber-Zombie.md) (including [Raiders](Enemies#tabber-Raiders.md)) will respawn.
File:RainEnds.png.md

#### Note[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Rain during Winter—is replaced by a Blizzard, which slows both players and zombies who are not in cover.

File:Thunder.png.mdLightning Strike

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- A violent type of weather that produces lightnings—the giant sparks of electricity from the sky.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- After day 20, a thunderstorm can occur and a lightning will strike randomly around the map (often hits the zombies.)

- If a [zombie](Enemies#tabber-Zombie.md) is hit, it will become charged (increased walkspeed.)

- If it hits a player, that player will be damaged and slowed down for 2 seconds.

### Seasons[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=3.md)]

These are currently the 3 seasons in the game, and changes every 50 days, alternating between Summer, Fall, and Winter.

SummerFallWinter

File:SummerSeason.png.mdSummer Season

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- Summer or Spring is the default season, which can be first witnessed after joining a new game.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- None

File:FallSeason.png.mdFall Season

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- Fall (or autumn) is a transitional period between summer and winter, where orange leaves fall from the trees.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- None

File:WinterSeason.png.mdWinter Season

#### Description[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- Winter is the coldest of the three seasons, bringing snow to the whole map.

#### Effects[[edit](Weather?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- The [Elemental](Enemies#tabber-Elemental.md) zombies mutate into [Frost Elementals](Enemies#tabber-Frost_Elemental.md).

- Rain during this season—is replaced by a Blizzard, which slows both players and zombies who are not in cover.

- [Farm Plots](Farm_Plot.md) grow crops slower during the Winter
