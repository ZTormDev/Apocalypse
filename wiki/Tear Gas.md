# Tear Gas

## Overview[[edit](Tear_Gas?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Tear Gas** is a type of [throwable](Looting#Tear_Gas.md) that can be found anywhere on the [map](Map.md). Players can throw it from a medium-long distance. It produces a large white smoke that slows down and weaken non-boss [enemies](Enemies.md) that walk into it.

File:Tear_gas.png.md

## Details[[edit](Tear_Gas?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Slows down non-boss [enemies](Enemies.md).

- Makes all enemies take more damage.

- Has a delay of 2.5 seconds, and a duration of 30 seconds.

## Notes[[edit](Tear_Gas?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Can also be found in [Barrels](Looting#Barrel.md), [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md), and on the ground.

- The Tear Gas was previously added in the [Military Area (Part 2)](Changelog.md) Update, but was later removed and added back in [Blighter & QoL Changes](Changelog.md) Update.

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.
