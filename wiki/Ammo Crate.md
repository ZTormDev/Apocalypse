# Ammo Crate

## Overview[[edit](Ammo_Crate?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Ammo Crate** is a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires  scrap and  batteries which scales depending on the player count, and can only be crafted once. When placed in the base, players can interact with it to craft ammo at the cost of 5 scraps per craft.

The amount of ammo given depends on which ammo you decided to craft:

- 24  Pistol

- 30  Medium

- 15  Long

- 10  Shells
