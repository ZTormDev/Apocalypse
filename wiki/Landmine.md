# Landmine

## Overview[[edit](Landmine?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Landmine** is an building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap and batteries. The landmine can be placed in the ground. When a zombie touches the landmine it detonates causing a huge explosion that deals high damage in a big area.
