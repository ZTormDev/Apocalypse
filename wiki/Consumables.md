# Consumables

## Overview[[edit](Consumables?action=edit&section=1.md)]

These are items that players can consume to gain different buffs and regenerate health or hunger. These items are found around the map in [Buildings](Locations#Buildings.md), in [Supply Drops](Supply_Drops.md), at the [Bandit Outpost](Bandit_Outpost.md), and out in the open. **Carrots** can only be found by placing [Farm Plots](Farm_Plot.md).
