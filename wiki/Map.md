## Overview[[edit](Map?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Map** can be crafted by players using the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md). It only requires a few scraps to make, and allows players to press **M** (on PC) to access a map of their current area. The range of the map increases as the [Generator](Day_1#Generator.md) levels up.

## Notes[[edit](Map?action=edit&section=5.md)]

- Press M on PC to show the Map.

- Tap the Map button on the top right of your screen on mobile to show the Map.

- Press the left button on the D-pad on console to pull up the Map.
