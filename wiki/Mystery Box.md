# Mystery Box

## Overview[[edit](Mystery_Box?action=edit&section=1.md)]
File:Mystery_Box.png.mdMystery Box.
File:TheMysteryBoxHasVanished.png.md"The Mystery Box has vanished.."
File:ShadowMuscle.png.mdThe Shadow Muscle

The Mystery Box is a type of crate that appears once per [location](Locations.md), which are the [Police Station](Locations#Police_Station.md), [Hospital](Locations#Hospital.md), or one of two [Bandit Outposts](Bandit_Outpost.md). Players can spend 150  to open it and obtain a random [gun](Weapons#Guns.md).

The Mystery Box can be opened until a dark zombie head appears, which makes the Mystery Box vanish and be replaced with the [Shadow Muscle](Enemies#Shadow_Muscle.md), emitting a shadowy aura.

After defeating the [Shadow Muscle](Enemies#Shadow_Muscle.md), the Mystery Box will reappear/spawn again at the same/different location after a certain amount of days has passed.

## Notes[[edit](Mystery_Box?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Only in this crate can you get the [Grenade Launcher](Weapons#tabber-Grenade_Launcher.md)
