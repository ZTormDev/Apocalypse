# Military Turret

## Overview[[edit](Military_Turret?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Military Turret** is a building that a player can obtain from the [Military Base](Locations.md) after a Military Raid. It is located on the second floor, by the [Commander Zombie](Zombies.md). When placed, much like a regular [Turret](Crafting_Table.md), it will automatically shoot at nearby enemies. It has infinite ammo, is much stronger and has a larger range compared to a regular [Turret](Crafting_Table.md). However, it shoots slower, despite the ammo being piercing (can pass through one enemy, deal damage and then hit another).

## Notes[[edit](Military_Turret?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be repaired using the [Repair Drone](Crafting_Table.md).

- [Zombies](Zombies.md) can damage it.

- Up to 4 can be placed in addition to the maximum number of regular [Turrets](Crafting_Table.md), no matter the player count.
