# Farm Plot

## Overview[[edit](Farm_Plot?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Farm Plot** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap and allows a player to grow 3 [carrots](Consumables#tabber-Hunger.md) every 10 minutes. These provide a food source. Only 15 can be crafted.

- Carrot can be grown into [Golden Carrots](Consumables#tabber-Hunger.md) when using [Farmer Class](Class#tabber-Farmer.md).

File:Farm_plot_no_carrots.png.mdCarrots will start growing after placing down the farm plot.
File:Farm_plot_with_carrots.png.mdCarrots grow after 10 minutes.

## Notes[[edit](Farm_Plot?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- The player does not need to do anything for the [carrots](Consumables#tabber-Hunger.md) to grow, they will grow automatically.

- Harvest the carrots as soon as they spawn to reset the timer. Timer will not reset otherwise.

- Grow slower during Winter
