**Old Nuclear Reactor**

## Overview[[edit](Nuclear_Reactor?action=edit&section=1.md)]

This is a timed dungeon-esq where [Zombies](Zombies.md) spawn in three waves. On the third wave, a boss called the [Experiment](Zombies.md) will appear. This area is located in the [Irradiated Zone](Irradiated_Zone.md). Once found by a player, it will appear on the map for everyone as a yellow and purple icon. The area is also filled with [Crates](Looting.md), [Emerald Crates](Currencies.md), and stand-alone [Loot](Looting.md).

### Brief description[[edit](Nuclear_Reactor?action=edit&section=1.md)]

It has similar HP, size and speed to a Brute. It will spawn after 3 waves of normal zombies during the reactor and killing it will open the door to the loot.

### Attacks[[edit](Nuclear_Reactor?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Direct attack with its arm when the player gets close to it.

- Radioactive Blast: The Boss shoots green projectiles from its arm, easy to dodge.

- Stomp: Similar to the Earthquake attack of the Brute, red circles will appear on the ground that can stun a player if standing on them.
