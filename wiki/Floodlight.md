# Floodlight

## Overview[[edit](Floodlight?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Floodlight** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap and batteries. It will produce a beam of light.

File:Floodlights_night.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Floodlight?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)

- Zombies target the floodlight when in range and will attempt to destroy it before most buildings if they can reach it. If a player gets too close, the zombies will change their focus to the player.
