# Traveling Merchant

## Overview[[edit](Traveling_Merchant?action=edit&section=1.md)]
File:Merchant.png.mdTraveling Merchant.

The **Traveling Merchant** is an NPC that appears randomly at the beginning of a day and leaves at the start of the next day. He has supplies that players can buy using , earned by killing Zombies, not Bandits. The item selection is global, which means that the selection is the same for all players. Additionally, once an item is sold out for one player, it is sold out for all players. The merchant has a [Pistol](Weapons#Pistol.md) that he will use to fend off any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that come near him. 

When the merchant appears, a message will appear at the top of the screen saying "A traveling merchant has arrived!".

File:MerchantArrived.png.md"A traveling Merchant has arrived!"

On the following day, a message will appear saying "The traveling merchant has left!".

File:MerchantLeft.png.mdThe Traveling Merchant has left!
