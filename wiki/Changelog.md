# Changelog

### Changelog[[edit](Changelog?action=edit&section=1.md)]

This page contains all updates and patch notes for **Survive the Apocalypse**.  
The most recent changes will always appear at the top.  

## July 2026 - 3 Seasons[[edit](Changelog?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Seasons: The season changes every 50 days, alternating between Summer, Fall, and Winter

- New Elemental Variant: Frost

- New Craftable: Mortar

- New Craftable: UV Light

- Rain during Winter is replaced by a Blizzard, which slows both players and zombies not in cover

- Broken cars can be destroyed using weapons

- Flamethrower DoT increased

- Zombies get 5% damage per 100 days on Hard Mode

- Farm plots grow crops slower during the winter
