## Overview[[edit](Cave?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Cave** is a dungeon that can be raided after reaching day 5. After that reaching at least 5 days, the dungeon will automatically open. Once entering, there will be [zombies](Enemies#Zombies.md) waiting inside, and the [Harbinger](Enemies#Harbinger.md) Boss in the middle. The [Harbinger](Enemies#Harbinger.md) Boss has a staff, and will attack players with it by firing spells and summoning [zombies](Enemies#Zombies.md). After these enemies are cleared, players can loot the the reward room once it opens. The dungeon will remain open, but will respawn the [zombies](Enemies#Zombies.md) and the [Harbinger](Enemies#Harbinger.md) Boss after 15 minutes.
