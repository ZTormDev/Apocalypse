# Gate

## Overview[[edit](Gate?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Gate** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap. When closed, the gate won't allow anything ([Zombies](Zombies.md), [Survivors](Survivors.md) or players) to pass through.

File:Gate.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Gate?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)

- [Crawlers](Zombies.md) and [screamers](Zombies.md) can climb over gates

- Less durable than [Fences](Fence.md) (will break more easily)
