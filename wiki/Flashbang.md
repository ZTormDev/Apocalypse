# Flashbang

## Overview[[edit](Flashbang?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Flashbang** is a type of [throwable](Looting#Flashbang.md) that can be found anywhere on the [map](Map.md). Players can throw it from a medium-long distance to stun non-boss [enemies](Enemies.md).

File:Flashbang.png.md

## Details[[edit](Flashbang?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Has a delay of 2 seconds.

- Stuns non-boss [enemies](Enemies.md) for 6 seconds.

## Notes[[edit](Flashbang?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Can also be found in [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md), [Supply Crates](Supply_Drops.md), and in the [Military Base Raid](Military_Base_Raid.md), and on the ground.

- Cannot stun players.

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.
