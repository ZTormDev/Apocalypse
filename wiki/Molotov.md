# Molotov

## Overview[[edit](Molotov?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Molotov** is a type of [throwable](Looting#Tear_Gas.md) can be crafted at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scraps to make. Players can throw it from a medium-long distance to deal area damage and gets [enemies](Enemies.md) burning.

File:Molotov_apoc.png.md

## Details[[edit](Molotov?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Breaks upon impact.

- has a duration of 10 seconds (slowly becomes smaller in size as [enemies](Enemies.md) are hit).

## Notes[[edit](Molotov?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Can also be found in [Barrels](Looting#Barrel.md), [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md), and on the ground.

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.
