## ***Infection***[[edit](Hard_Mode?action=edit&section=2.md)]
File:Infection_Tracker.png.mdInfection Tracker

***Infection*** is a progressive difficulty mechanic and shows clearly by the Infection Tracker. As the Tracker percentage increases, the game world becomes increasingly hostile. This system is designed to avoid delaying objectives or playing inefficiently results in a tangible escalation of threat.

The Infection Tracker given to players after release ***Pathogen***, 2 stat which is

- ***Infection***: This is a percentage bar that show how much Pathogen affection atmosphere around, it shows chance to spawning Infested zombies.

- Active Nodes: Active Node are ******Infestation******, it will randomly spawn between 2-3 days when surviving in Hard Mode, it would gradually increasing ***Infection***.

  - The more active ***Infestation***, the faster the ***Infection*** increase speed.

  - Once Active Nodes are all cleared to 0, The ***Infection*** naturally reduced very slow.

  - ***Infestation*** is available to headshot by attacking the cap part of the Mushrooms.

  - ***Infestation*** spawns like how the supply crates spawn working, and somehow can spawn at roads 
 sometimes.

 can only see only Supply Crate or the Node
