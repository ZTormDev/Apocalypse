# Time Machine

## Overview[[edit](Time_Machine?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Time machine** is a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap, batteries and zombie hearts. When placed the players gain +1 day multiplier (like reparing a [Power Plant](Locations.md)).

File:Time-machine.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Time_Machine?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- The time machine is expensive and quite fragile so it should be defended properly.

- The max placement limit is 3 for all amounts of players in a run, whether its a solo or a group of 5 players.
