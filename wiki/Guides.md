### Team Composition[[edit](Guides?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- SOLO

Stalker OR Operator OR Commander

- DUO

Commander + Operator OR Stalker + Commander

- TRIO

Operator + Commander + Stalker OR Operator + Commander + Psychic

- 4-Player

Engineer + Commander + Stalker + Operator

- 5-Player

Engineer + Commander + Stalker + Operator + Psychic
