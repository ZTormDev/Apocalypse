# Grenade

## Overview[[edit](Grenade?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Grenade** is type of [throwable](Looting#Grenade.md) that can be crafted at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scraps and a battery. Players can throw them from a medium-long distance to deal area damage.

File:Grenade_apoc.png.md

## Details[[edit](Grenade?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Takes 3 seconds to explode.

- Does up to 90 damage to the player throwing the Grenade.

## Notes[[edit](Grenade?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Can also be found in [Barrels](Looting#Barrel.md), [Supply Crates](Supply_Drops.md), [Military Box](Military_Box.md), and on the ground.

- Used as [Ammo](Looting#Ammo.md) for the [Grenade Launcher](Weapons.md)

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.
