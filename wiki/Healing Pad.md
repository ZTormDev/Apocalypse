# Healing Pad

## Overview[[edit](Healing_Pad?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Healing Pad** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scraps and batteries to be crafted. It can be used to heal players.

## Notes[[edit](Healing_Pad?action=edit&section=2.md)]
