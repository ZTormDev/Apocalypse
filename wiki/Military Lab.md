# Military Lab

## Overview[[edit](Military_Lab?action=edit&section=1.md)]

This area is a sub-area within the [Military Base](Military_Base.md). It is a lab where players can craft the **Power Armor**.

## Power Armor[[edit](Military_Lab?action=edit&section=2.md)]

This is the highest level armor a player can have in the game. It is craftable at the **Prototype** Station. It gives more defense and each time a player is hit by a zombie it absorbs the hit. Once enough hits are absorbed, when a Zombie hits the player again, it will bounce the zombie off similar to a forcefield. 

File:Prototype.png.mdPrototype Station

## Crafting[[edit](Military_Lab?action=edit&section=3.md)]

To craft the Power Armor, players must have the three necessary pieces. The pieces are Chest and two arms. These can be found on Zombies **ONLY** in the Military Base.

- Unlock the [Military Base](Military_Base.md)

- Find the Military Lab

- Kill Zombies wearing each piece of the armor.

- Take the armor pieces to the **Prototype** Station

- Drag the pieces into each area. Arm pieces on the left and right and the Chest piece in the center.

- Lightning will flash as the Power Armor is made

- Equip onto player
