# Military Box

## Overview[[edit](Military_Box?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The Military Box is a type of [breakable](Looting#Breakables.md) that spawns within the [Military Area](Locations#tabber-Military_Base.md).
Players can break it for a chance to randomly get [Compounds](Consumables#Compound.md), [Bandages](Consumables#Bandage.md), [Medkits](Consumables#Medkit.md), [ammos](Looting#Ammo.md) or [throwables](Weapons#Throwable.md). The ammount of loots are random.

File:Military_Box.png.md

- 64 Pistol 

- 120 Medium 

- 50 Long 

- 50 Shells 

- [Medical Items](Consumables.md) (Compound I, Compound R, Compound S, Medkit, Bandage)

- [Throwables](Weapons#Throwables.md) (Grenade, Flashbang, Tear Gas, Molotov)

## Notes[[edit](Military_Box?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- There are always 14 Military Boxes in the [Military Base Raid](Military_Base_Raid.md), and respawns everytime the Raid reopens.

- It serves a purpose similar to wooden [barrels](Looting#Barrel.md) which are both breakable and drops loots.

- It shows its name as "barrel" when pinged.
