# Military Base Raid

## Overview[[edit](Military_Base_Raid?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The is a timed dungeon that can be done every 5 day cycles. The players first need to destroy the 2 turrets on top of the walls, then they need to open the main door to start the raid. After that, a group of zombies will appear inside which the players must defeat. To enter the actual base building, the garage door needs to be opened. When they do, the [Exterminator](Enemies#Exterminator.md) appears with 2 [Muscles](Enemies#Muscle.md). After these enemies are cleared, the players can loot the main building of the military base.
