# Turret

## Overview[[edit](Turret?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Turret** is a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap and batteries. When placed the turret automatically shoots at nearby enemies and it has infinite ammo.

File:Turret.png.md

## Placement Limits[[edit](Turret?action=edit&section=2.md)]

There is a maximum amount of **Turrets** that can be placed at a time that is determined by the amount of players in the game as shown in the following table:

| Players | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | 8 turrets |
| 2 | 8 turrets |
| 3 | 12 turrets |
| 4 | 12 turrets |
| 5 | 16 turrets |
