## Overview[[edit](Crafting_Table?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Crafting Table** (or Crafting Bench) is a utility station that allows players to use scrapped materials to craft useful items. There are 4 levels to the crafting table that require specific materials to unlock each level. The amount of materials needed to unlock each level and to craft the items on the table—scale with the amount of players in the game. The materials are Scrap , Batteries , and Zombie Heart
