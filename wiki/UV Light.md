# UV Light

## Overview[[edit](UV_Light?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **UV Light** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap and batteries. It will produce a beam of ultra violet light that slows zombies that are in front of it.

## Notes[[edit](UV_Light?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)

- Zombies will target the closest UV Light when there are no players nearby. If there are players closer than the UV Light they will prioritize attack the players rather than the UV Light.
