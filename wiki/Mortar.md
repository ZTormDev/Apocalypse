# Mortar

## Overview[[edit](Mortar?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Mortar** is a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap and batteries. When placed the mortar automatically fires explosives at nearby enemies and it has infinite ammo.
