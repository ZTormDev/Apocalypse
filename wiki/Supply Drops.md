# Supply Drops

## Overview[[edit](Supply_Drops?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Supply Drops are [Events](Events.md) in the game that occur every 5 days, directly before a [Blood Moons](Events.md). On the Map the icon is a dark brown, square box. [Zombies](Zombies.md) surround the drop. Clear them to open it. Drops scale to the amount of players in game, as well as the amount of days survived. Supply crates have a chance to be contested by [Raiders](Bandit_Outpost.md) instead of zombies on day 60+
