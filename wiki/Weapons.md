## Overview[[edit](Weapons?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Weapons are tools players can use to fight [Enemies](Enemies.md). Weapon types are Melee, Gun, and Throwable. Weapons spawn based on their rarity type. Weapons can be found around the map in [Crates](Looting.md), [Barrels](Looting#Barrel.md), and in the open. They can also be found in [Supply Drops](Supply_Drops.md). 

Weapons will have different outline colors depending on the rarities. The higher the rarity of a weapon, the more powerful it is.

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.

## Notes[[edit](Weapons?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- [Elementals](Enemies#Elemental.md) are immune to the Molotov or any related fire damage

Flashbang

File:Flashbang.png.md

  
*See the [Flashbang](Flashbang.md) page for more information.*

- This is a non-lethal explosive device designed to temporarily blind and stun enemies with a flash of light.

- Cannot be crafted. Can only be found across the [map](Map.md) and in [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md).

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.

Tear Gas

File:Tear_gas.png.md

 
*See the [Tear Gas](Tear_Gas.md) page for more information.*

- This is a small canister that releases gas that slows down and weaken enemies.

- Cannot be crafted. Can only be found across the [map](Map.md).

- The path of the throwable is visualised when you aim it.

 Weakening Potion

File:WeakeningPotion.png.md

*See the [Potions](Potions.md) page for more information.*

- This can be crafted in the Brewing Stand using the Alchemist class.

- This potion can be crafted using 7 Rotten Flesh and 2 Bloater Cores in the Brewing Stand.

- Once thrown, it produces a purple radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will have their max HP reduced, will deal less damage to the player and will continuously take minor damage.

 Acid Potion

File:AcidPotion.png.md

*See the [Potions](Potions.md) page for more information.*

- This can be crafted in the Brewing Stand using the Alchemist class.

- This potion can be crafted using 6 Rotten Flesh and 1 Spitter Heart in the Brewing Stand.

- Once thrown, it produces a green radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will continuously take significant damage.

 Ice Potion

File:IcePotion.png.md

*See the [Potions](Potions.md) page for more information.*

- This can be crafted in the Brewing Stand using the Alchemist class.

- This potion can be crafted using 8 rotten flesh, 2 bloater core, and 2 tainted steel in the Brewing Stand.

- Once thrown, it produces a blue radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will be frozen and will continuously take small damage.
