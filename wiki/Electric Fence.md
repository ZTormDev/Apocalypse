# Electric Fence

## Overview[[edit](Electric_Fence?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Electric Fence** its a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scrap and bateries. This is a defense structure similar to the [Fence](Fence.md). Additionally [Zombies](Zombies.md) receive damage when in contact with this structure.
