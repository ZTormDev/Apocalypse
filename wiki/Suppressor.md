# Suppressor

## Overview[[edit](Suppressor?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Suppresor** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 3. It requires scraps and the player can use them on almost every fire arm to reduce the acoustic intensity of the gunshot.

File:Suppressor_apoc.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Suppressor?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Suppressors can only be crafted 10 times for each player.

- This item is also needed to recruit the [Assassin](Class#Assassin.md)

- Can't be equipped on:

  - [Double Barrel](Weapons#tabber-Double_Barrel.md)

  - [LMG](Weapons#tabber-LMG.md), [Heavy Sniper](Weapons#tabber-Heavy_Sniper.md)

  - [Explosive Crossbow](Weapons#tabber-Explosive_Crossbow.md), [Ray Gun](Weapons#tabber-Ray_Gun.md), [Flamethrower](Weapons#tabber-Flamethrower.md), [Minigun](Weapons#tabber-Minigun.md), [Grenade Launcher](Weapons#tabber-Grenade_Launcher.md)

  - [Ion Shotgun](Weapons#tabber-Ion_Shotgun.md), [Freeze Ray](Weapons#tabber-Freeze_Ray.md), [Railgun](Weapons#tabber-Railgun.md)

  - [MediGun](Weapons#tabber-MediGun.md) and [Heavy Revolver](Weapons#tabber-Heavy_Revolver.md).
