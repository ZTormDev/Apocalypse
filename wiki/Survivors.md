# Survivors

## Overview[[edit](Survivors?action=edit&section=1.md)]

These are NPCs that appear randomly on the [map](Map.md) on days 6, 35, and 75. The player can interact with the Survivor to have it follow them or leave it at the base for it to go looting during the day. Armor can be dragged onto the Survivors. Better [Armor](Accessories.md) will replace the current armor.
