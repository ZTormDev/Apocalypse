## Overview[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Events are limited-time happenings that give affect to all players in the game. There are two types of events: Regular Events and AA (Admin-Exclusive) Events. 

## Regular Events[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=2.md)]
Blood MoonSupply DropBandit Raid

File:Bloodmoon-2.png.mdBloodmoon night.

This is an event that happens every 5 days right after the Supply Crate drop. The 5x multiplier from repairing the Powerplants expedites the Blood Moons occurrence. The hordes of [Zombies](Zombies.md) during the Blood Moon are exponentially more difficult and more in number than other days. Brutes can also appear in Blood Moons. This will occur after day 50. The last zombie of a Blood Moon will also drop a Zombie heart which is needed to craft certain level Crafting Tables and certain items on the table.  

File:Bloodmoon-1.png.md"Bloodmoon will rise tonight".

File:Suply-crate.png.mdSuply Crate.

Supply Drops happen directly before the Blood Moon. The message "A supply crate is being dropped! Check your map!" will appear across the top of the screen. Click on the Map to see where the Crate will fall. It is a dark brown square. These are crates that fall from the sky every 5 days right before the Blood Moon. The crate is filled have better loot than what is typically found around the map such as: Refined Fuel, Refined Metal, Weapons, Armor, Bandages, Medkits, MRE’s, etc. Clear the zombies surrounding it, and then press E to open it. 

File:Suply-crate-2.png.md"A supply crate is being dropped! Check your map!".

File:Raider-attack2.png.mdRaider Raid.

On occasion, raiders will come to fight your camp instead of zombies. If this happens, the game will let you know in text on your screen that says "A hostile faction is preparing to attack tonight!". The first time a Bandit Raid occurs a cutscene will play, the cutscene will not play again after the first Bandit Raid has occurred. These are non-zombie NPC’s that have powerful Melee weapons (Fire Axe, Sledgehammer), and guns. You clear these Raiders just as you would clear Zombies. These NPC’s will drop ammo. 

File:Bandit-attack.png.md"A hostile faction is preparing to attack tonight!"

## Admin-Exclusive Events[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=3.md)]
Emerald ZombiesEmerald RainSupply DropZombie King AttackGun Boost

File:EmeraldZom.png.mdEmerald Zombie.
File:EmeraldZom-not.png.md"Emerald Zombies will attack tonight!".

This is an Admin Abuse exclusive event and consist of a normal wave with x amount of zombies depending on the day and a few **Emerald Zombies**.

- They have the size and speed of a Basic Zombie, but are much tankier.

- They drop 1 emerald per player. For example, in a 3 player game the **Emerald Zombie** will drop 3 [Emeralds](Currencies.md) and each player will be able to collect only one of them.

File:EmeraldRain.png.mdAfter an Emerald Rain.

This is an Admin Abuse exclusive event where [Emeralds](Currencies.md) fall from the sky for the player to collect. 

- All the players on the game can collect the same [Emeralds](Currencies.md), they wont disappear for everyone after one player collect them.

- The [Emeralds](Currencies.md) will remain on the map until all players collect them or the game ends.

File:Supply-crates.png.mdMap after a Supply Crates Event!  

This is an Admin Exclusive event where multiple supply drops fall from the sky at the same time. 

- Supply Crates will remain on the map until a player open them or the games ends, same thing with the loot the player gets after opening one.

File:SupplyCrates-not.png.mdSupply Crates are raining from the sky!

File:ZombieKing.png.mdZombie King Attack.
File:ZombieKing-not.png.mdThe Zombie King has brainwashed raiders to attack your base tonight!

This is an event where both zombies and raiders will attack your base. The bandits that attack your base will have purple eyes instead of white. Otherwise this is a mix of a normal zombie attack and a bandit raid

File:YourGunsFeelStronger.png.mdYour guns feel stronger...

The **Gun Boost** is an event that occurs when the Admin of the game decides to enhance every [guns](Weapons#Guns.md) across all servers.

All [guns](Weapons#Guns.md) receive:

**+50%** Capacity (rounded up)

**+25%** Damage

**+25%** Firerate

**+25%** Accuracy

### Notes[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- [Guns](Weapons#Guns.md) with this boost are entirely incompatible with the [Weapon Upgrader](Weapon_Upgrader.md).

## Limited Events[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=4.md)]
Blood Gem RainBlood HarvestBlood Tomb Raid

File:BloodGemRain.png.mdAfter a Blood Gem Rain.

This is an Admin Abuse exclusive event where Blood Gems fall from the sky for the player to collect. 

- All the players on the game can collect the same Blood Gems, they wont disappear for everyone after one player collects them.

- The Blood Gems will remain on the map until all players collect them or the game ends.

- Only happened during the Blood Harvest Event.

### Notes[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- This is a limited event that lasted a week and is no longer in game.

- Only available from **May 2, 2026** to **May 9, 2026**.

File:BloodharvestNPC.png.mdBlood Harvest NPC.

- This event features the new limited [Stalker](Class#tabber-Stalker.md) class.

- The Blood Tomb was introduced during this event, which you could raid for Blood Gems or by completing daily quests.

### Notes[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- This is a limited event that lasted a week and is no longer in game.

- Only available from **May 2, 2026** to **May 9, 2026**.

Blood Tomb Raid
File:Blood_Tomb.png.mdThe Blood Tomb in the graveyard.
File:Bloodtombraid.png.mdEnemies waiting inside the tomb.

- This is a dungeon located on the upper right of the [Map](Map.md).

- The Blood Tomb contains zombies including the [Skeleton](Enemies#tabber-Skeleton_Runner.md) and the [Skeleton King](Enemies#tabber-Skeleton_King.md).

- You need to raid the [Blood Tomb](Locations.md) and defeat the [Skeleton King](Enemies#tabber-Skeleton_King.md) to be rewarded 20 Sealed Blood Gems which are directly placed in the inventory (escaping is required to claim the Blood Gems).

- The tomb opens every 3 hours globally. After the 3 hours you have a 30 minute window where the raid will open in new games or ones that haven't already raided the tomb. If you already started a run during those 30 minutes, then the tomb will stay open indefinitely. You can only do the Blood Tomb Raid once per run.

### Notes[[edit](Events?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- This is a limited event that lasted a week and is no longer in game.

- Only available from **May 2, 2026** to **May 9, 2026**.
