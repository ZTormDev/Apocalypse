# Repair Drone

## Overview[[edit](Repair_Drone?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Repair Drone** is a building that a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 4. It requires scrap and batteries. When placed the **Repair Drone** will heal nearby buildings periodically. There is a maximum amount of **Repair Drones** that can be placed at a time that is determined by the amount of players in the game.

## Placement Limits[[edit](Repair_Drone?action=edit&section=2.md)]

There is a maximum amount of **Repair Drones** that can be placed at a time that is determined by the amount of players in the game as shown in the next table:

| Players | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | 4 repair drones |
| 2 | 4 repair drones |
| 3 | 5 repair drones |
| 4 | 5 repair drones |
| 5 | 5 repair drones |
