# Fence

## Overview[[edit](Fence?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Fence** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap and blocks [Zombies](Zombies.md) when placed. It can be used to protect the base.

File:Fence.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Fence?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)

- [Crawlers](Zombies.md) and [screamers](Zombies.md) can climb over

- More durable than [Gates](Gate.md)
