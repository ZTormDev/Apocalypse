# Shelf

## Overview[[edit](Shelf?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Shelf** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap and allows a player to store items. The types of storable items are: [Carrots](Looting#Carrots.md), [Weapons](Weapons.md), Food, etc

## Notes[[edit](Shelf?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Can be destroyed by [Zombies](Zombies.md)
