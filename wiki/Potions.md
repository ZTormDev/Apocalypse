## Overview[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Potions are items crafted by using the Brewing Stand of the [Alchemist](Class#tabber-Alchemist.md) class. Each potion gives different effects and are made using ingredients obtained from killing various zombies.

File:AcidPotion.png.mdThe Acid Potion

### Ingredients[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- 6x rotten flesh

- 1x spitter heart

### Effects[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Once thrown, it produces a green radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will continuously take significant [Damage](Stats.md)

- Every time an enemy takes damage from the radius, it shrinks slightly

- Lasts 10 seconds

- Does not affect players or [Survivors](Survivors.md)

File:IcePotion.png.mdThe Ice Potion

### Ingredients[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- 8x rotten flesh

- 2x bloater core

- 2x tainted steel

### Effects[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Once thrown, it produces a blue radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will be frozen and will continuously take [Damage](Stats.md)

- Lasts 9 seconds

- Does not affect players or [Survivors](Survivors.md)

File:WeakeningPotion.png.mdThe Weakening Potion

### Ingredients[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- 7x rotten flesh

- 2x bloater core

### Effects[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Once thrown, it produces a purple radius where any [Enemies](Enemies.md) that enter will have their max HP reduced, will deal less damage to the player and will continuously take minor [Damage](Stats.md)

- Lasts 9 seconds

- Does not affect players or [Survivors](Survivors.md)

File:RecoveryCompound.png.mdThe Recovery Compound

For more information, please see the [Consumables](Consumables.md) page.

### Ingredients[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- 10x rotten flesh

- 1x nuclear rod

- 1x spitter heart

### Effects[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Only obtainable through the brewing stand

- Regenerates the player's [Health](Stats.md) continuously for 13 seconds after consumption

File:CompoundR.png.mdThe Rage Compound

For more information, please see the [Consumables](Consumables.md) page.

### Ingredients[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- 3x screamer claw

- 8x spitter heart

### Effects[[edit](Potions?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Increases [Melee](Weapons.md) damage

- Increases melee speed

- Player heals with every melee kill

- Lasts 120 seconds
