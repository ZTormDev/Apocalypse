# Ammo Box

## Overview[[edit](Ammo_Box?action=edit&section=1.md)]
File:Ammo-box.png.mdAmmo Box

The Ammo Box is an item that can be found in [Supply Crates](Supply_Drops.md) when opening them, and in the [Nuclear Reactor](Locations.md) upon eliminating all 3 waves. They can alternatively be purchased from the [Traveling Merchant](Traveling_Merchant.md).

- You'll find up to a maximum of 6 Ammo Boxes in supply crates

- You'll always find 4 Ammo Boxes in the nuclear reactor

## Extra Info[[edit](Ammo_Box?action=edit&section=2.md)]
File:Ammo-box-info.png.mdAmmo amounts you get in the Ammo Box

The amount of ammo you get in each Ammo Box doesn't scale regarding the number of players or the amount of days you open them at.

The amount of ammo you get is in the picture to the right and also as follows:

- Long ammo - 30

- Shells - 30

- Pistol ammo - 64

- Medium ammo - 90
