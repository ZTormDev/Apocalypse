# Military Base

File:OpenMilitaryGate.png.mdOpening Gate

## Overview[[edit](Military_Base?action=edit&section=1.md)]

This area is a zone on the map that is highlighted blue and usually on the top left. It will require a [Military Keycard](Looting.md) that can be obtained from [Crates](Looting.md) around the [Map](Map.md). There are several entrances and all require the Keycard to open. Upon opening the door, a message will appear at the top of the screen that says "User opened the Military Zone!". The Military Base will contain strong [Zombies](Zombies.md), Legendary [Crates](Looting.md), and a [Military Lab](Military_Lab.md) where players can craft Power Armor. There will also be cosmetic differences such as different vehicles like ambulances and tanks, as well as different Zombie types.
