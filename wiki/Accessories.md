## Overview[[edit](Accessories?action=edit&section=1.md)]

These are items you can find in **Crates**, **Supply Drops**, or scattered around the map. Each accessory improves the player's stats; the rarer the item, the better the stats.

## Backpack[[edit](Accessories?action=edit&section=2.md)]
Default BackpackGood BackpackGreat Backpack

Default Backpack

File:Default-backpack.png.md

- Common Tier

- Obtained on the start of the game.

- All players start with this backpack.

- It allows a player to store items with 10 inventory space.

- Cannot be scrapped .

Good Backpack

File:Good-backpack.png.md

- Uncommon Tier

- Can be found in [crates](Looting#tabber-Crates.md), and [supply drops](Supply_Drops.md).

- It allows a player to store items with 20 inventory space.

- Can be scrapped for 16  (only if all players on the team have equal or better backpacks.)

Great Backpack

File:Great-backpack.png.md

- Epic Tier

- Can be found in [crates](Looting#tabber-Crates.md), and [supply drops](Supply_Drops.md).

- It allows a player to store items with 30 inventory space.

- Can be scrapped for 32  (only if all players on the team have equal or better backpacks.)

## Wearables[[edit](Accessories?action=edit&section=3.md)]
Light ArmorMedium ArmorHeavy ArmorPower ArmorGas MaskBirthday Hat

Light Armor

File:Light-armor.png.md

- Uncommon Tier

- Can be found in [buildings](Locations.md), [crates](Looting#tabber-Crates.md), and [supply drops](Supply_Drops.md).

- Grants players light defense

- Can be Scrapped for 16  if all players on the team have equal or better versions.

- Can be worn by [Survivors](Survivors.md) (drag the armor for them to equip.)

Medium Armor

File:Medium-armor.png.md

- Rare Tier

- Can be found in [buildings](Locations.md), [crates](Looting#tabber-Crates.md), and [supply drops](Supply_Drops.md).

- Grants players medium defense

- Can be Scrapped for 24  if all players on the team have equal or better versions.

- Can be worn by [Survivors](Survivors.md) (drag the armor for them to equip.)

Heavy Armor

File:Heavy-armor.png.md

- Epic Tier

- Can be found in [buildings](Locations.md), [crates](Looting#tabber-Crates.md), and [supply drops](Supply_Drops.md).

- Grants players heavy defense

- Can be Scrapped for 32  if all players on the team have equal or better versions.

- Can be worn by [Survivors](Survivors.md) (drag the armor for them to equip.)

Power Armor

File:PowerArmor-1.png.md

- Legendary Tier

- Crafted at the [Military Lab](Military_Lab.md)

- Grants players heavy defense and faster HP regeneration.

- Stun and knock-back zombies after they hit you (10s cooldown).

- Can be Scrapped for 40  if all players on the team have equal or better versions.

- Can be worn by [Survivors](Survivors.md) (drag the armor for them to equip.)

Gas Mask

File:Gasmask.png.md

- Common Tier

- These masks are obtained from the [Hazmat](Zombies.md) Zombie. Upon kill, these zombies have a chance to drop a mask. Press E to equip it. The mask will appear on your face and allow players to go into the “Irradiated Area”

- Cannot be scrapped
**Note:** The gas mask is currently unobtainable since the [The Forest (Part 1) Update](Changelog#The_Forest_(Part_1).md).

Birthday Hat

File:BirthdayHat.png.md

- Unknown Tier

- Added when it was the Admin's birthday

- Limited during the [Admin-Exclusive Event](Events#Admin-Exclusive_Events.md)

- The admin spawns it whenever they want

- No special use/effects, strictly just a cosmetic
