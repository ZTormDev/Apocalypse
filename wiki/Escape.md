## Overview[[edit](Escape?action=edit&section=1.md)]

Players have the ability to escape after at least 100 days survived. Escaping ends the run after players climb up the rope into the helicopter. 

## Requirements[[edit](Escape?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- 4/4 Powerplants Repaired

- 1/1 Radio Tower Part

- Survive to Day 100 **at least**

## Requirements Met[[edit](Escape?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- A Message will appear on the screen once the Radio Tower part has been taken to the Radio Tower.
File:Radiopower-message.png.mdThe Radio Tower is active!

- Once repairing all Power Plants and fixing the Radio Tower this message will appear at the bottom of the screen
File:Radio_Message.gif.md

- A Flare will appear in base

- Majority of players **must** Vote Yes to the flare
File:Flare.png.mdVoting Flare at base.

- Survive 5 minutes until the helicopter gets to your base

- Once the helicopter is there, players will need to get on the rope

File:Escaping.png.mdClimbing the rope.
File:Helicopter-1.png.mdEscaping in the helicopter.

- 15  will be rewarded to each player who escapes. Escaping is still possible even while dead, that is if your body is near the rope.
