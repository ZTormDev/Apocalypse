File:Powerplant.png.mdPower Plant

- There are 2 Regular Power Plants around the map, accessible at levels 2 and 3 of the [Generator](Day_1.md). Fixing these is necessary to [Escape](Escape.md) at day 100.

File:Betterpowerplant.png.mdBetter Power Plant

- There are 2 Better Power Plants around the map, accessible at levels 4 and 5 of the [Generator](Day_1.md). They are bigger versions of the Regular Power Plants, with more [Loot](Looting.md) and more [Zombies](Zombies.md). [Zombie Electrics](Zombies.md) exclusively spawn here. Fixing these is necessary to [Escape](Escape.md) at day 100.

File:Radio_Tower.png.mdRadio Tower

- Players can find [Barrels](Looting.md), [Crates](Looting.md), [Consumables](Consumables.md), and more. You must place the [Radio Tower Part](Bandit_Outpost.md) here if you want to escape.

- The **Forest** is a zone/biome that is located at the bottom right of the [Map](Map.md). It is a green-highlighted zone on the [Map](Map.md). It does not require anything to be able to enter.

- This zone contains an overgrown city and a big forest at the same time. There are different smaller areas here such as cabins, logging camps, and the [Cave](Cave.md) –having different loots.

File:Irradiated_Zone.png.mdThe Irradiated Zone

- This area will have strong zombies and damage players who enter without equipping a gas mask.

- If a player enters the player without a mask a message will appear on the screen that says "This air is radioactive! You need a [Gas Mask](Accessories.md) to survive.

- Loot will be better in this area.

- The [Nuclear Reactor](Nuclear_Reactor.md) is located here.

[Nuclear Reactor](Nuclear_Reactor.md)
File:Reactor.png.mdNuclear Reactor

- This is a dungeon-esq part of the map located in the Irradiated Zone (the green highlighted area on the map).

- The icon is purple and once you find it, it will be highlighted on the map.

- The Reactor consists of waves of zombies with one final boss called the [Experiment](Zombies.md). Once cleared the wave & the [Experiment](Zombies.md) 15m cooldown will start countdown for the next raid.

### Notes[[edit](Locations?action=edit&section=1.md)]

- Have been removed and replaced by The [Cave](Cave.md) after The Forest Part 1 update
