## Overview[[edit](Weapon_Upgrader?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Weapon Upgrader** is a utility station that allows players to enhance the stats (e.g., damage, firerate, ammo capacity) or modify the perks of their [guns](Weapons#Guns.md) and/or [melees](Weapons#Melee.md). It is located exclusively within the normal zone of the [Level 5 Generator's](Map#Level_5.md) Range. Before players can use it, the machine must first be repaired using the [Wrench](Looting#tabber-Tools.md), a [tool](Looting#tabber-Tools.md) that can only be obtained by successfully completing the [Military Base Raid](Military_Base_Raid.md). Once repaired, the station allows players to spend 500  to upgrade an equipped [weapon](Weapons.md).

## Notes[[edit](Weapon_Upgrader?action=edit&section=3.md)]

- Weapons can only hold one active modification at a time, meaning any new rolls will permanently replace the existing upgrade.

- Weapons with exclusive [Admin Gun Boost](Events#tabber-Gun_Boost.md) are entirely incompatible with the **Weapon Upgrader**.

- A weapon that already has an upgrade cannot be boosted by the [Admin Gun Boost](Events#tabber-Gun_Boost.md).
