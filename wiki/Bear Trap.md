# Bear Trap

## Overview[[edit](Bear_Trap?action=edit&section=1.md)]

A **Bear Trap** is an item a player can craft at the [Crafting Table](Crafting_Table.md) Level 2. It requires scrap and stuns [Zombies](Zombies.md) that step into it for a short period of time.

File:Bear_trap.png.md

## Notes[[edit](Bear_Trap?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Once a bear trap has been used, it will need to be rearmed (this can be done by going up to it and interacting with it)

- The [Hunter](Class#Hunter.md) Class can automatically rearm them. Additionally they do more damage.
