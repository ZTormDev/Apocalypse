# Crate

### Overview[[edit](Crate?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Crate** is a regular type of [crate](Looting#tabber-Crate.md) that appears as a rectangular-shaped metal box, it servers as a vital loot source for players looking to gather weapons, ammunitions, and survival gears. The **Crate** can spawn with different tiers as shown on the table below.

### Spawn Behavior:[[edit](Crate?action=edit&section=2.md)]

- Everywhere on the [Map](Map.md) (except the player's base)

Guaranteed Building Spawns:
The following specific buildings have a guaranteed chance.

- [The Cave](Cave.md)

- [Military Base](Military_Base_Raid.md)

- [Hospital](Locations#tabber-Hospital.md)

- [Police Station](Locations#tabber-Police_Station.md)

- [Power Plant](Locations#tabber-Power_Plant.md)

- [Military Zone](Military_Base.md)

- [Radio Tower](Locations#tabber-Radio_Tower.md)

- [Bandit Outposts](Bandit_Outpost.md)

- [Gas Stations](Locations#Gas_Station.md)
