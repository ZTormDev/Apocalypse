# Barrel

## Overview[[edit](Barrel?action=edit&section=1.md)]

The **Barrel** is a type of [breakable](Looting#tabber-Breakables.md) that spawns almost anywhere on the [Map](Map.md).
Players can break it for a chance to randomly get [Compounds](Consumables#Compound.md), [Bandages](Consumables#Bandage.md), [Medkits](Consumables#Medkit.md), [ammos](Looting#Ammo.md) or [throwables](Weapons#Throwable.md) (the ammount of loots are random).

- It serves a purpose similar to [Military Boxes](Military_Box.md), which are both breakables and drop loots.
